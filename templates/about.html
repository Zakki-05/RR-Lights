About RR Light House

Lighting up lives in Pernambut since 2010.

What started as a small electrical supply store has grown into Pernambut's premier destination for lighting and electrical solutions. At RR Light House, we don't just sell products; we provide brightness, safety, and style for your homes and businesses.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted household name for electrical solutions in the region, known for quality and innovation.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, energy-efficient, and affordable electrical products while maintaining exceptional customer service.

Our Journey

2010

Established as a small electrical shop on Main Road, Pernambut, focusing on basic wiring and bulbs.

2015

Expanded inventory to include decorative fancy lights, chandeliers, and premium switch gears.

2020

Partnered with major brands like Phillips, Havells, and GM to become an authorized trusted dealer.

Today

Proudly serving over 5000+ happy customers with the largest lighting showroom in the region.
